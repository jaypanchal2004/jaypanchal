Questism


"Questism" is an RPG where Ray, a character, is accidentally teleported to a creepy alternate realm due to a failed quantum mechanics experiment. To escape, Ray must complete various quests, with a tracker displaying progress. Completing all quests grants a wish, influencing the game’s outcome. The game blends mystery, exploration, and decision-making for a unique adventure.




Third Year Project

System for our college

IEMS


Internal Evaluation Management System (IEMS): A web-based system developed to digitize and automate the college's manual paperwork for internal evaluations. It includes features like automated report generation and the ability to conduct MCQ-based exams, similar to Google Classroom. The system streamlines administrative tasks, saving time and improving efficiency. Approved and praised by professors for its innovation and usefulness.It solves problems in traditional paper based processes, like lost assignments, delayed grading, manual errors, and lack of transparency. By automating these tasks, it creates a smoother, more efficient, and secure way to manage academic activities. IEMS helps modernize education by making operations faster and more reliable, improving communication between students and teachers, and keeping all data safe. It’s a valuable tool for making academic management easier and more effective.





Intermediate Project

Hospital Management System


The HMS is a comprehensive system designed to streamline the operations of a hospital. It facilitates the management of patient records, appointment scheduling, doctor schedules, billing, and inventory. The system was developed to replace traditional manual processes, improving efficiency and accuracy. It provides an easy-to-use interface for both hospital staff and patients, allowing for quick access to information and seamless management of hospital operations. This project aimed to enhance the quality of healthcare services and reduce administrative overhead.
